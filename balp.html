About

BALP — the Big Arse Loose Party Band — is exactly what it sounds like. A collective of Fremantle musicians who believe a gig should feel like a celebration, not a performance.

Born out of a shared love for the songs that make rooms move, BALP brings together decades of live experience and an absolute refusal to take themselves too seriously.

If you've seen us, you know. If you haven't — come find out.
